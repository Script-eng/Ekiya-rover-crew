EKIYA PROGRAM

Our mission is to educate young people to play a constructive role in society. As Ekiya Rover Crew, we bring together alumni from Starehe Boys and Girls who are now in universities and colleges to continue the scouting tradition in a higher education setting.

We focus on personal development, community service, and building leadership skills among our members while staying true to scouting principles and values. Our activities are designed to create well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

ADVENTURE

Ekiya is driven by the spirit of exploration of new challenges, encouraging resilience, curiosity, and joy of discovery through camps, hikes, and outdoor activities.

LEADERSHIP

Ekiya emphasizes guiding with integrity, inspirational leadership, and empowering others within and beyond the crew through mentorship and training.

SERVICE

Ekiya is about giving back to the community, developing a sense of responsibility and a deeper connection with the world around us through various service projects.
