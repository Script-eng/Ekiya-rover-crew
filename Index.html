SCOUT PROGRAM

The scout program is a combination of exciting activities, nature exploration. Children try their hand at competitions and relay races. They get to know nature, gaining survival skills and knowledge from professional instructors.

The event is suitable for both beginner campers and experienced hikers. The staff will customize the activities to suit the abilities of each age group of participants. And all this in the most comfortable conditions of a real tent camp.

CAMP FEATURES

TENT CITY

Accommodation is organized in a tent city, where we get to the nature as close as possible and forget about the internet and urban hustle and bustle.

TOURISM

Learning the basics of tourism is the most beloved part of the program. We learn how to prepare firewood, build a fire, extract water and navigate the stars.

CAMPFIRE MEALS

There are 2 types of food in the camp: regular food (in bad weather conditions) and campfire meals (perhaps the most favorite dishes of all children).
