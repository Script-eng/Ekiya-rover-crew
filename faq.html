FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01.

HOW IS ONE ELIGIBLE TO JOIN THE CREW?

To join Ekiya Rover Crew, you need to be an alumnus of Starehe Boys or Girls Centre, currently in university or college, and between the ages of 18-26 years. We welcome those who are interested in continuing their scouting journey in a higher education setting and are committed to our values of adventure, leadership, and service.

02.

WHAT DOES EKIYA DO?

Ekiya Rover Crew engages in a variety of activities centered around our core principles. We conduct community service initiatives, run long-term projects such as Children's Home Fundraising, Holistic Sexual Health and Support, and Student Mentorship. We also organize in-crew activities like Secret Santa, camps, hikes, town clean-ups, and volunteering opportunities both locally and internationally. Our focus is on personal development, leadership training, and positive community impact.

03.

HOW CAN I SUPPORT EKIYA ROVER CREW?

There are several ways to support Ekiya Rover Crew:
- Donate to our projects and initiatives
- Volunteer your time and expertise for specific activities
- Provide mentorship to our members
- Partner with us for community service projects
- Offer internship or employment opportunities for our members
- Share our mission and activities with your network
- Provide resources or facilities for our events and meetings

04.

HOW IS EKIYA ROVER CREW FUNDED?

Ekiya Rover Crew is funded through a combination of member contributions, fundraising activities, donations from well-wishers, partnerships with organizations that share our values, and grants from relevant institutions. We maintain transparent financial practices and ensure that all funds are used efficiently to support our mission and activities.

05.

WHAT DOES THE NAME "EKIYA" MEAN?

The name "Ekiya" represents our values and aspirations as a rover crew. It symbolizes unity, strength, and forward movement - all central to our mission of continuing the scouting tradition while making a positive impact in society. The name was chosen to reflect our unique identity within the broader scouting community.

06.

DO I NEED PREVIOUS SCOUTING EXPERIENCE TO JOIN?

While most of our members have previous scouting experience from their time at Starehe, it is not a strict requirement. What's more important is a commitment to our values and a willingness to participate in our activities. If you're a Starehe alumnus within the age range and interested in what we do, you're welcome to join regardless of your previous scouting background.

07.

HOW OFTEN DOES THE CREW MEET?

The Ekiya Rover Crew holds regular meetings, typically monthly for general assembly and more frequently for committee and patrol meetings. We also organize periodic camps, hikes, and community service activities throughout the year. While we encourage consistent participation, we understand the demands of university life and provide flexibility for members to balance their academic commitments with crew activities.

08.

CAN I JOIN IF I'M STUDYING IN A DIFFERENT CITY OR COUNTRY?

Yes, we welcome Starehe alumni studying in different cities or countries. While physical attendance at all activities may not be possible, we facilitate remote participation through online meetings and provide opportunities for international members to contribute to projects and initiatives. We also encourage members studying abroad to represent Ekiya in international scouting events and to initiate community service in their locations.

09.

WHAT SKILLS CAN I DEVELOP AS A MEMBER OF EKIYA?

As a member of Ekiya Rover Crew, you can develop a wide range of skills:
- Leadership and organization
- Project management
- Public speaking and communication
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Problem-solving and critical thinking
- Outdoor and survival skills
- Community service and social responsibility
- Networking and relationship building
- Cultural awareness and global citizenship
We provide a supportive environment for personal and professional growth.

10.

HOW DO I STAY UPDATED WITH EKIYA'S ACTIVITIES?

You can stay updated with Ekiya's activities by:
- Following our social media accounts (Instagram: starehe_ekiyarovercrew, Facebook: Starehe Ekiya Rover Crew, TikTok: Starehe_ekiyarovercrew)
- Subscribing to our newsletter
- Regularly checking our website for announcements
- Joining our WhatsApp or Telegram groups (for members)
- Attending our open events
We strive to maintain transparent and consistent communication with both members and the wider community.
