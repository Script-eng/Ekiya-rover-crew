OUR STRUCTURE

The Ekiya Rover Crew is structured to promote effective leadership, clear communication, and distributed responsibility. Our organizational framework allows us to efficiently manage our activities, foster collaboration, and maximize our impact in the community.

THECOUNCIL

The Council serves as the highest strategic and decision-making body within the Ekiya Rover Crew. Comprising elected leaders, it provides overall direction, oversees long-term planning, ensures alignment with our mission and vision, and represents the Crew in key external engagements, particularly with the Starehe community and major partners.

THECOH

(Court of Honor) The operational heart of the Crew, responsible for implementing Council decisions and managing day-to-day activities. It typically includes patrol leaders and committee heads, ensuring effective coordination and communication between units. The COH translates strategy into action.

THEPATROLS

Patrols form the foundational units of Ekiya, fostering camaraderie and teamwork. Each patrol, led by a Patrol Leader, comprises a small group of members. This system encourages peer leadership, facilitates closer bonds, allows for focused participation in activities, and provides a direct channel for member feedback.
