ABOUT EKIYA

Ekiya members at community service

Our Story

Ekiya Rover Crew was established by alumni from Starehe Boys and Girls Centre who wanted to continue the scouting tradition beyond secondary school. As university and college students between the ages of 18 and 26 years, we recognized the need for a platform that would allow us to maintain our scouting values while addressing the unique challenges and opportunities of higher education.

The name "Ekiya" embodies our commitment to unity, strength, and forward movement. Our crew brings together individuals from various disciplines and backgrounds, united by their shared experience at Starehe and their dedication to making a positive impact in society.

The Ekiya Rover Crew has been instrumental in my personal and professional development. Through our community service initiatives and leadership opportunities, I've gained skills that complement my academic education and prepared me for future challenges.
- Jane Doe, Founding Member

MISSION & VISION

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate young people to play a constructive role in society. We achieve this by providing a supportive environment where Starehe alumni can continue to develop their leadership skills, engage in meaningful community service, and pursue personal growth through adventure and exploration.

We are committed to upholding the values instilled in us at Starehe while adapting them to the context of higher education and young adulthood. Through our activities and initiatives, we aim to create well-rounded individuals who are equipped to make positive contributions to their communities and the world at large.

Our Vision

Our vision is creating a better world. We envision a society where young people are empowered to lead positive change, where service to others is valued as highly as personal achievement, and where the scouting principles of duty to God, duty to others, and duty to self guide individual and collective action.

We aspire to be a model rover crew that inspires similar initiatives among alumni of other institutions, creating a network of young adults committed to service, leadership, and lifelong learning. Through our example and outreach, we hope to demonstrate the enduring relevance of scouting values in addressing contemporary challenges.

OUR PRINCIPLES

At Ekiya Rover Crew, we are guided by three core principles that shape our activities, decision-making, and interactions with the broader community. These principles reflect our commitment to holistic development and positive social impact.

ADVENTURE

Ekiya is driven by the spirit of exploration and new challenges. We encourage resilience, curiosity, and the joy of discovery through various activities that push our members outside their comfort zones. From challenging hikes to innovative community projects, adventure helps us develop adaptability and problem-solving skills.

LEADERSHIP

Ekiya emphasizes guiding with integrity and inspirational leadership. We believe in empowering others within and beyond the crew, developing leaders who lead by example and inspire positive change. Our leadership structure provides multiple opportunities for members to develop and demonstrate their leadership abilities.

SERVICE

Ekiya is about giving back to the community and developing a sense of responsibility. Through our service initiatives, members develop a deeper connection with the world around them and recognize their potential to make a positive impact. We believe that service to others is not just an activity but a way of life.

OUR JOURNEY

2019

Foundation

Ekiya Rover Crew was founded by a group of Starehe alumni who wanted to continue their scouting journey beyond secondary school.

2020

First Projects

The crew launched its first community service projects, establishing partnerships with local organizations and institutions.

2021

Expansion

Membership grew significantly, allowing for the formation of multiple patrols and specialized committees to handle different aspects of the crew's activities.

2022

Long-term Projects

The crew initiated its signature long-term projects, including Children's Home Fundraising, Holistic Sexual Health, and Student Mentorship.

2023

International Engagement

Ekiya began participating in international scouting events and established connections with rover crews from other countries.

2024

Digital Transformation

The crew embraced digital tools to enhance communication, project management, and outreach, launching its official website and expanding its social media presence.
