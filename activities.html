COMMUNITY SERVICE

01

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

We carry out community service within our local communities with the assistance of other rover crews and community members. Our goal is to make a positive impact on society and develop a sense of responsibility among our members.

02

CHILDREN'S HOME FUNDRAISING

One of our long-term projects involves fundraising for children's homes to provide essential resources and support. This initiative helps create a nurturing environment for children who need it most, while teaching our members about compassion and social responsibility.

03

HOLISTIC SEXUAL HEALTH AND SUPPORT

This long-term project focuses on providing education and support around sexual health. We create safe spaces for discussions, provide accurate information, and connect individuals with appropriate resources when needed.

04

STUDENT MENTORSHIP PROJECT

We engage with young scouts through training, mentoring, and fun activities. Our members serve as role models and provide guidance to help younger scouts develop their skills, confidence, and leadership abilities.

IN-CREW ACTIVITIES

05

SECRET SANTA

During the Christmas season, we organize a Secret Santa event where crew members appreciate one another by exchanging gifts. This tradition strengthens our bonds and creates a festive atmosphere of gratitude and fellowship.

06

CAMPS AND HIKING

We organize regular camps and hiking expeditions to complete our badges while encouraging resilience, curiosity, and the joy of discovery. These outdoor adventures allow members to connect with nature, learn survival skills, and build camaraderie.

07

TOWN CLEAN UPS

As part of our commitment to community service, we organize town clean-up activities where members work together to beautify public spaces and raise awareness about environmental conservation.

08

VOLUNTEERING

We create opportunities for our members to volunteer within and outside the country, allowing them to gain international exposure, learn from diverse cultures, and develop their skills for personal growth.
