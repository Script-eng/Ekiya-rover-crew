OUR PROJECTS

Ekiya Rover Crew is committed to making a positive impact on our communities through long-term, sustainable projects. Our initiatives focus on addressing real needs, creating lasting change, and providing opportunities for our members to develop leadership skills while serving others.

Children's Home Fundraising

Active Since 2021

Our Children's Home Fundraising project aims to provide essential resources and support to children's homes in and around Nairobi. We work with selected homes to identify their needs, organize fundraising events, and coordinate volunteer efforts to improve the living conditions and educational opportunities for the children.

Beyond material support, we also focus on building lasting relationships with the children through regular visits, mentorship, and recreational activities. Our goal is to create a nurturing environment where these children can thrive despite challenging circumstances.

Holistic Sexual Health and Support

Active Since 2022

The Holistic Sexual Health and Support project addresses the need for comprehensive and accurate information about sexual health among young people. Through workshops, discussion forums, and informational resources, we create safe spaces for conversations about topics that are often stigmatized or misunderstood.

We partner with health professionals and educators to ensure that the information we provide is accurate, age-appropriate, and delivered in a respectful manner. The project also includes a support component, connecting individuals with appropriate resources when needed.

Student Mentorship Project

Active Since 2022

Our Student Mentorship Project connects Ekiya members with younger scouts, providing guidance, support, and inspiration. As former Starehe students, we are uniquely positioned to understand the challenges and opportunities faced by current students and to help them navigate their educational and personal development journey.

The mentorship program includes regular one-on-one sessions, group activities, career guidance, and academic support. We work closely with school scout units to ensure that our efforts complement their existing programs and address the specific needs of their scouts.

Environmental Conservation Initiative

Planning Launch: 2024

Our upcoming Environmental Conservation Initiative will focus on addressing environmental challenges through education, advocacy, and direct action. The project will include regular clean-up activities, tree planting, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with environmental organizations.

We believe that scouts have a special responsibility to protect and preserve the natural world. This initiative will provide opportunities for our members to fulfill this responsibility while developing their understanding of environmental issues and sustainable practices.
